Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the cost estimates?

expand_more

Our estimates are designed for preliminary budgeting and planning purposes. They are based on standardized, up-to-date market rates for materials and labor in India. However, actual costs can vary significantly based on your specific location, choice of brands, quality of materials, and the contractor you hire.

Is the DreamHome Calculator free to use?

expand_more

Yes, all the calculator tools available on our platform are completely free for personal use.

Can I use these estimates to finalize a contract?

expand_more

We strongly advise against it. This tool should be used as a reference to get a ballpark figure. Always obtain detailed, itemized quotations from multiple professional contractors before finalizing any contract.
